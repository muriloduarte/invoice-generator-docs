Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 5, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to the Privacy Policy of Invoice Generator. We take your privacy seriously and are committed to protecting your personal information. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data when you use our extension.

2. Information We Collect

The Invoice Generator extension collects and processes the following information:

  • Invoice Data: Information you manually enter when creating invoices, including:
    • Sender data (name, address, contact information)
    • Customer data (name, address, contact information)
    • Invoice items (description, quantity, prices)
    • Dates and invoice numbers
  • Configuration Preferences: Your language, currency, and display format preferences
  • Templates: Custom invoice templates you create or modify

3. How We Use the Information

The collected information is used exclusively to:

  • Generate invoices according to your specifications
  • Save your data locally for future use
  • Personalize your extension experience
  • Allow exporting invoices in PDF or other formats

Important: We do not use your information for advertising, marketing, or any other purpose unrelated to the core functionality of the extension.

4. Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's storage API (chrome.storage.local). This means that:

  • Your information is never sent to external servers
  • No data is transmitted over the internet
  • You have full control over your data
  • Data remains in your browser and can be deleted at any time

5. Data Sharing

We do not share any personal information with third parties. The extension operates completely offline and has no integration with external services that could access your data.

The only exceptions are when you voluntarily:

  • Export an invoice and share it with third parties
  • Save an invoice to cloud services of your choice

6. Data Security

We have implemented security measures to protect your information:

  • Data is stored locally using secure Chrome APIs
  • We do not collect unnecessary sensitive data
  • The extension does not request excessive permissions
  • The extension code follows security best practices

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: View all data stored by the extension
  • Modify: Edit or update your information at any time
  • Delete: Remove all data stored by the extension
  • Export: Backup your data in JSON or PDF format

To exercise these rights, you can use the built-in features in the extension or uninstall it, which will automatically remove all stored data.

8. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

The extension does not use cookies or tracking technologies. We do not collect information about your browsing behavior or internet usage.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. When we make significant changes, we will notify you through an update in the extension description on the Chrome Web Store and update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

10. Contact

If you have questions, concerns, or requests related to this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@invoicegenerator.com
  • GitHub: github.com/muriloduarte/invoice-generator-docs

11. Legal Compliance

This policy complies with:

  • General Data Protection Law (LGPD) - Brazil
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) - European Union
  • Chrome Web Store Developer Program Policies
