Privacy Policy

Updated: October 2025

This Privacy Policy explains what information the Invoice Generator Chrome extension processes when you use it, why we do so, and how your data is handled.

I. Data processed by the extension

The extension stores only your invoice settings and preferences (e.g., hourly rate, hours per day, company/project data, holiday selection, PDF language) in your browser using chrome.storage.sync (with localStorage as a fallback). No invoices or personal text you type are sent to any servers.

Your data is used locally to calculate totals and generate PDFs. No account system or login is used.

II. Exchange rate requests

To assist with USDâ†’BRL conversion, the extension may request the current rate from economia.awesomeapi.com.br. We do not send any personal data in this request; it is a simple GET and we only receive public quotation data. If it fails, a local fallback rate is used.

III. Technical and usage information

  • We do not collect analytics or telemetry.
  • The extension may detect the browser/interface language to initialize translations.
  • It uses the "tabs" permission only to open the settings page or this privacy page in a new tab when you click relevant links.
  • The extension does not request host permissions and does not read or modify the content of websites you visit.

IV. Cookies and local storage

We do not set cookies. Preferences are stored in chrome.storage.sync (synced with your Google account when available) or in localStorage as a fallback. You can delete this data at any time by uninstalling the extension or by clearing the extension site data in your browser.

V. Your rights and contact

You can access, review, or delete the data saved by this extension at any time. All information remains locally in your browser's storage. For questions, concerns, or feature requests, contact:

  • Email: muriloduartegoncalves@gmail.com
  • Chrome Web Store: Install / view extension

VI. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Relevant changes will be reflected by updating the date at the top of this page.
